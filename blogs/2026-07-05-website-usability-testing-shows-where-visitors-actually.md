Website usability testing is the simplest way to stop guessing about what visitors understand. You watch real people attempt real tasks and note where they slow down, backtrack, hesitate, or abandon the page. That makes evaluating navigation effectiveness concrete because the test shows whether labels, section order, and page cues match visitor intent.
