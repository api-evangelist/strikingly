Website homepage messaging is the first explanation visitors receive when they land on your site. Before they scroll, compare pages, or click a button, they are trying to answer a few quiet questions: what does this business do, is it for me, why should I care, and what should I do next? Good website homepage messaging reduces the effort required to understand the business.
