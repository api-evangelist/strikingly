A digital purchase experience is the full buying path from product interest to order confidence. It includes product detail, cart clarity, delivery expectations, payment choices, confirmation, and support. A store can have strong products and still lose buyers if checkout feels unclear, payment choices feel narrow, or small surprises appear late.
