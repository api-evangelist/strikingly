Online customer experience is the feeling visitors get while they move through a website, ask questions, compare options, and decide whether to return. It includes page clarity, speed, navigation, forms, support access, follow-up, and trust. A site can look attractive and still create a frustrating online customer experience if each step asks the visitor to work too hard.
