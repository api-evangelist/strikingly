A website optimization framework keeps improvement work from becoming a pile of random ideas. Without a framework, a team may change a headline because someone dislikes it, move a button because another site does it, or redesign a page before knowing what is actually weak. A practical website optimization framework connects four habits: prioritizing impactful improvements, measuring performance consistently, testing changes systematically, and supporting continuous growth.
